Stage Pass - Music Listening

Stage Pass creates a new level of intimate concert experience in an 8-bit world without the big expense, crowds, and unpredictability of an in-person event. It does this by connecting users and artists in curated listening rooms and events.

Problem Statement

People want safer, more accessible, more cost effective options for concert going and listening to music with others, especially during public health crises like Covid-19.

Objectives & Goals

-Create a new level of intimate concert experience with an 8-bit avatar.

-Reduce big expenses, crowds, and the unpredictability of an in-person event.

User Persona

Eric Nguyen is a full-time student who enjoys attending concerts, new technology, and exploring new music. He has felt isolated during the pandemic and represents the typical user of Stage Pass.
