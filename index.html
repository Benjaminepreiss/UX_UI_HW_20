Stage Pass - Music Listening

Stage Pass creates a new level of intimate concert experience in an 8-bit world without the big expense, crowds, and unpredictability of an in-person event. It does this by connecting users and artists in curated listening rooms and events.

Problem Statement

People want safer, more accessible, more cost effective options for concert going and listening to music with others, especially during public health crises like Covid-19.

Objectives & Goals

-Create a new level of intimate concert experience with an 8-bit avatar.

-Reduce big expenses, crowds, and the unpredictability of an in-person event.

Taskflows

Based on our user research, we identified three primary users: listener, host, and verified artist. We then built out user flows based on tasks associated with the user type.

The listener user flow focuses on completing the sign up process, creating an avatar, and joining a stage.

All users of Stage Pass have the ability to host their own stage.

The host user flow focuses on creating and customizing a stage, inviting friends and creating a setlist.

Verified Artists are notable account holders who can host featured stages called Events. These users receive special customization options and can incentivize engagement through unique digital merchandise.

The Verified Artist user flow focuses on creating and customizing an Event.
